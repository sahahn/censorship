SYNOPSIS:

This bill would prohibit public K-12 schools

9

and public institutions of higher education from

10

teaching certain concepts regarding race or sex,

11

such as critical race theory.

12

This bill would prohibit a K-12 school or

13

public institution of higher education from

14

imposing any penalty upon or discriminating against

15

a student on account of his or her refusal to

16

support, believe, endorse, embrace, confess, act

17

upon, or otherwise assent to divisive concepts,

18

such as critical race theory.

19

This bill would also authorize a public K-12

20

school or public institution of higher education to

21

promote racial, cultural, or ethnic diversity or

22

inclusiveness, provided the efforts of the school

23

or institution are consistent with the requirements

24

of this bill.
